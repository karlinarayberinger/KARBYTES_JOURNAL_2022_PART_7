The following precepts are part of a futuristic society. I think such rules are quite humane..

1. Each female-bodied human may complete up to two pregnancies in which at least one live offspring is born before being surgically sterilized (and a female-bodied human may only complete one pregnancy after adopting at least one orphaned minor if there are any orphan minors remaining).

2. Any male bodied human may adopt an orphaned minor before impregnating any female-bodied human.

3. Any human is allowed to medically alter one’s physiology such that the human phenotypically or even genetically resembles the opposite sex. (What this means is that men can legally transform into women which have functional female reproductive organs and genitalia and women can legally transform into men which have functional male reproductive organs and genitalia).

4. No pregnancy is allowed to be completed unless each one of the female-bodied human involved consents to that pregnancy’s completion.

5. Any genome is public domain (which means any heritable traits are free to use in the production of “test tube babies”).

6. If no humans want to become pregnant, then no human will be forced to become pregnant.
