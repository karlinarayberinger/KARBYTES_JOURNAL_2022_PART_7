KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_151

The following note exists to make up for the fact that I was not able to record these insights in time before I got distracted and wrote what I thought were more urgent, important, and socially acceptable notes instead the morning after I did the strongest shrooms I could find…

Approximately one month ago, I did penis envy mushrooms for the first time. Allegedly, the penis envy strain is the most powerful psychedelic mushroom strain on the market. I orally congested 7 grams of the stuff in one sitting while sitting outside underneath a starry sky overlooking the Bay Area from the vantage of the dead children’s memorial which is about half a mile northeast of the John George Psychiatric Hospital. I also consumed some edible cannabis about half an hour prior to consuming the shrooms. It was approximately 10PM when I took the shrooms.

As the trip started to intensify, I was bullied by thought police who talked to me as though I were some unruly teenager who was being reckless, wasting money, and making my parents worry about me because I was not at home when it was past 10PM and I was not inside of some kind of safe building. While I was being interogated and yelled at by those thought police, I noticed what seemed to be hallunitory effects from the shrooms which I thought made the whole trip worthwhile and which I failed to describe in as much detail as I really want to until now…now that I am up on a hill far away from the general public on a steep and secluded trail away from the more crowded outdoor recreation areas in Castro Valley. (Aside: Environment is respobsible for more than 50% of how much range of thought and idea expression I have access to)….

As my shroom trip approached peak intensity, I noticed that my rendering of reality seemed to be coming out of nowhere as discrete frames rather than as a single seamless space-time continuum. Each one of those frames felt like something I birthed out of my body with tangible effort and straining. Each frame felt like me answering “yes” to the following question: “Are you willing to keep on investing energy towards your survival?” I felt horrified at the prospect of not being able to rest. I felt that I was forever tricking myself; lying to myself that I only had a small and finite number of frames to go before I would get the chance to relax and not feel anymore suffering…only to discover that just as I get close to completing those frames, I had more to go (and I was being informed at a deeper level of awareness that the struggle would go on forever (and that endless struggle was one of infinitely many types of universes I could be instantiating and experiencing from a partial and limited perspective)).

I look forward to doing more shrooms after I save up at least $300 in my bank account and after at least a few more weeks pass. I do not want to rush the process. I want to get the most bang for my buck and not waste the trip on processing junk data or on processing data which was left over from previous trips which I neglected to finished integrating into my visceral knowledge interface. The website cluster I have engineered into existence is an extension of my organic human nervous system and perhaps an informational bridge which will allow me to travel between multiple universes and multiple lifetimes. I gleefully look forward to condensing and refining my philosophical prose and computer science application code into gemlike “bricks” and inserting them into their designated web page slots. I feel that 100% of the gemlike content I want to keep already exists (or, at the very least, is prototyped and/or alluded to) within the current website cluster configuration. My “work” for the next few weeks (which I voluntarily assign myself to do) is to mine for those gems and to eliminate the junk so that only the gems remain. The final product (which I expect to be ready no later than 01_NOVEMBER_2022) will be a masterpiece of a website named Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com; a site which will have no extraneous fluff and will have as little essential content as needed to set my selves in motion towards being a happy and successful scientist, philosopher, engineer, and artist.
