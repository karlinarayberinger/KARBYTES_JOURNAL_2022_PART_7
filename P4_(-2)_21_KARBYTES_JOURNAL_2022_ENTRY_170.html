While walking uphill to my dad’s house in Castro Valley to get my tent, I got the idea to elaborate on why I condone making technologically assisted suicide unconditionally accessible to all adults in the utopian society which I attempted to outline in the web page named TECHNOCRACY in my primary website (whose name is Karlina Object dot WordPress dot com).

Rather than just emphasize making suicide legal and accessible to all people who may be depressed or otherwise motivated to end their lives, I want to include the possibility that human beings may remain conscious even after they appear to be dead (while their organs are still somewhat in tact).

Recently I read about a suicide pod in the news which allows the patient to click a button from inside of that suicide pod (which looks like a car and is about the size of a car) which causes nitrogen gas to fill that chamber. Rather than die a slow, agonizing death by poison or after one’s head its severed from its body, the nitrogen gas takes the place of oxygen molecules inside of that person’s bloodstream and the oxygen receptors which are present in each one of that human body’s cells; causing the person to fall asleep and enter brain death from oxygen deprivation. Rather than writhe in agony while being poisoned to death and unable to move due to poison-induced paralysis, the person who enters a deep sleep after being deprived of oxygen probably does not experience any pain. While in a deep sleep, the oxygen deprivation may be causing the person to hallucinate in a deep dream state which may or may not be heavenly or hellish or just unremarkable.

I think that suicide is useful for two purposes: (1) suicide allows a person to exit a life which that person finds to be insufficiently satisfying and (2) suicide allows a person to avoid the grusome slow, agonizing death which dying of “natural causes” is likely going to cause that person. By intentionally ending one’s own life, one is preventing the possibility of being tortured.

Eventually, the atmosphere on Planet Earth will cause excruciating agony for existing and yet to be born humans (unless humans seriously take actions to stop global climate destabilization and environmental toxicity).

Besides suicide, I wanted to mention to myself that voting by ballot is not necessary if a technocracy which surveils and manipulates each human being’s nervous system can decode what each person wants and influence what each person wants in accordance to what the technocracy deems to be beneficial (i.e. in accordance to its goals as a multifaceted and Internet of Things wide global Internet’s emergently conscious Artificial Intelligence).

I understand that a lack of psychological distance from one’s own body diminishes that person’s agency. The purpose of having a private structure in which to write is to avoid being manipulated into writing in a manner which is not authentic to my stylistic and informational content goals. In Castro Valley, it is hard to get a word in (even if I do not speak out loud) while other people simultaneously talk over me to drown me out and hypnotize me into feeling the emotions they want me to feel, noticing the sensory stimuli they want me to notice, and pay attention to words which are not my own.

The thought police are tirelessly working to subdue my excessively expensive thought processes for the comfort and safety of the people who do not have that kind of time, energy, and environmental advantages at their disposal.

Agency is a measure of how many options an information processing machine has while at a decision-making point.

If an information processing agent has infinitely many options, that information processing agent cannot finish computing what the best option would be using some algorithmic process of elimination.

If an information processing agent has exactly one option, that information processing agent is forced to implement that option as an action.

– DECISION(PERCEPTION)

– ACTION(DECISION)
